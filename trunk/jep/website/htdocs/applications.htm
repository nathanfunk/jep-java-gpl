DataScan - Scientific Image Analysis
Coordinator: Ken Ritley
http://dxray.mpi-stuttgart.mpg.de/site/software/datascan/DataScan.htm
DataScan performs double precision image analysis for Scanning Tunneling Microscopy (STM), Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM), x-ray and neutron diffraction, and other general types of microscopy.
More generally it can perform many topographical image analysis tasks, for many data analysis applications. DataScan is optimized to easily digitize the points in scanned images/contour plots of published scientific graphs!

Martello Tower
Head Programmer: Andy Davis
Martello Tower is a children's educational game written in the 1980's by Anita Straker for the BBC. In the 2nd year of our Computer Science degree, a group of students (including myself) reimplemented Martello Tower in Java.
At various stages throughout the game, the player has to solve puzzles to continue, one of the puzzles involves creating numbers by writing mathematical expressions that equate to the desired value. After much consideration, I used JEP to do this for me with ease and accuracy.
Although JEP has much more to it than parsing simple + - * / ( ) expressions, it was nevertheless perfect for that use. Without JEP, our project time could have been greatly increased and I am grateful for this. - [Andy Davis]
